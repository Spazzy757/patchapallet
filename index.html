Pallets • Boxes • Crates

Welcome to Patch A Pallet.

We specialize in manufacturing 2 way and 4 way entry pallets, Boxes and Crates to customers specifications Another service we offer is on-site packing. Where our experienced staff will come to your site and assist or pack your goods into the boxes and crates for you. We are proud to offer a fumigation service according to ISPM Specifications. The fumigation process takes place on our premises. All products are fumigated and stamped and a certificate is issued. As we manufacture pallets and boxes ourselves we have timber available on-site for direct sale to the consumer We buy and sell second hand pallets When a pallet needs a little bit of TLC to get back into running order, we offer a repair service on or off site depending on your requirements All orders delivered in Gauteng with possible arrangements in further provinces. As a relatively small business with over twenty years experience we understand the best service we can provide is customer service. We are a very hands on business which strives to meet all requirements with the best service possible. Please remember no job is to big or to small. We appreciate all orders!
